Papa Louis

Papa Louie opened Papa's Pizzeria October 9, 2007 in Tastyville, Flipverse. It was the first of many successful restaurants opened by Papa Louie all over Flipverse. He has many loyal customers who frequent his restaurants to eat his delicious food.

Roy

Roy is Papa Louie's nephew and first employee. Papa Louie left him in charge of the Pizzeria when he departed on one of his many adventures. As the sole employee of Papa's Pizzeria, Roy does all the cooking, waiting, and management at the restaurant.
